JotPenny Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

JotPenny is designed as a local-only expense tracker.

1. Information We Collect

The provider of JotPenny does not collect, receive, store, transmit, sell, or share your personal information or expense data through any server or online account system.

2. How the App Stores Data

Expense records, settings, and related app data are stored locally on your device. When you create a backup, the backup file is created only at your direction and saved to a location you choose through the system file picker.

3. Backups and Restores

Backup files are controlled by you. Because the provider does not collect or store your data, the provider cannot recover, inspect, restore, or verify your backups for you. You are solely responsible for managing and protecting your device data and any exported backup files.

4. Analytics, Tracking, and Advertising

JotPenny does not include third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies. The app does not use your data for tracking.

5. Third-Party Access

Your data is not shared with the provider. Data may only leave the app environment if you explicitly export a backup file using the system file picker or otherwise choose to share a file yourself.

6. Children's Privacy

JotPenny is not designed to collect personal information from children because the provider does not collect user data through the app.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in future versions of the app. If the policy changes materially, the updated version will be presented within the app.
